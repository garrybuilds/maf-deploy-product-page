Six agents. One deployed team.

Each agent handles a specific business function. Together, they run the repetitive work across your operations — 24/7.

Marketing Agent

Lead generation, outbound campaigns, content, local SEO. Sends follow-up sequences to unsold quotes. Monitors Google Business Profile and responds to reviews. Keeps your pipeline full without you thinking about it.

Sales Agent

Lead qualification, proposal drafting, CRM sync, follow-up sequences. Responds to new leads within 5 minutes. Drafts estimates from your templates. Sends quote follow-ups at 2 hours, 24 hours, and 72 hours. Turns inquiries into booked jobs.

Operations Agent

Scheduling, dispatch, job tracking, crew communication. Auto-assigns jobs based on location and skill. Sends crew notifications when schedules change. Tracks job completion and flags delays. Keeps your field teams moving and your office from drowning.

Customer Success Agent

Onboarding, check-ins, review requests, referral programs. Sends review requests within 24 hours of job completion. Runs re-engagement campaigns for customers who haven't called in 6+ months. Turns one-time jobs into lifetime customers.

Finance Agent

Invoicing, payment collection, expense tracking, cash flow forecasting. Sends invoices within 4 hours of job completion. Follows up on overdue accounts at day 7, 14, and 30. Reconciles payments in QuickBooks automatically. Flags at-risk accounts before they hit 60 days. Gets you paid faster and keeps the books clean.

Support Agent

Ticket routing, FAQ responses, escalation management. Routes incoming issues to the right person. Answers common questions automatically (scheduling, billing, status). Escalates complex issues to your team with full context. Handles the routine so your team handles the complex.
